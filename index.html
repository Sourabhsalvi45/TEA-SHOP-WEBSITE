About Our Tea House

Welcome to Shridhar's Tea House, an iconic establishment in Kolhapur since 2002. We take immense pride in serving the finest teas, coffees, and traditional beverages crafted with authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Our mission is to provide a warm, welcoming atmosphere where friends and families can gather to enjoy premium beverages at reasonable prices. Every cup is brewed with care and passion.
